Frequently asked questions

Is Freeunitconvert free?

Yes. Ad-free, no signup.

Which categories are supported?

Length, weight/mass, temperature, volume, area, speed, data size, time, pressure, energy, and more.

Does it handle temperature conversion correctly?

Yes. Temperature uses additive offset (not just multiplication), so Celsius to Fahrenheit converts correctly (e.g., 0°C = 32°F).

Does it support historical or niche units?

Common units are covered. For very specialized units, dedicated scientific tools may be better.

Does it work offline?

Yes. PWA-installable and fully offline after first load.

Can I convert currency?

Not currently. Currency conversion requires real-time rate data and is out of scope for a client-side tool.
