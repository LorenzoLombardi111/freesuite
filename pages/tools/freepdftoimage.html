How it compares

Smallpdf, iLovePDF, and similar services upload your PDFs to their servers. Freepdftoimage runs locally in your browser so files stay private. For batch PDF-to-image on hundreds of files, dedicated desktop tools may be more convenient. For individual PDFs, Freepdftoimage is the simplest option.

Privacy

PDFs are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Extracted images download directly to your device.

Frequently asked questions

Is Freepdftoimage free?

Yes. Free, unlimited, with no signup or watermarks.

What quality are the output images?

Configurable. Default is 150 DPI which is suitable for screens. Increase for print quality.

Can I choose PNG or JPG?

Yes. PNG preserves transparency and offers lossless quality. JPG gives smaller files for photographic content.

Are PDFs uploaded?

No. All processing is client-side in your browser.

Can I extract a single page?

Yes. After processing, download individual pages or all as a ZIP.

What PDF versions are supported?

Most standard PDF versions (1.4 through 2.0). Encrypted or password-protected PDFs need to be decrypted first.
