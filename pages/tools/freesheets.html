Free Online Spreadsheet — formulas and calculations without Google

A lightweight browser spreadsheet for formulas, running totals, and simple tables. Standard functions (SUM, AVG, IF, VLOOKUP, COUNT) supported. No account.

Features

  • Standard spreadsheet functions (SUM, AVG, IF, VLOOKUP, COUNT, MAX, MIN, etc.)
  • Cell references and formulas
  • Copy-paste to and from Excel or Google Sheets
  • Export to CSV or XLSX (Excel-compatible)
  • Sort and filter rows
  • Works offline after first load
  • No signup, no account

How it works

  1. Open freesheets.app
  2. Type data into cells like any spreadsheet
  3. Use formulas starting with = (e.g., =SUM(A1:A10))
  4. Export to CSV or XLSX when ready

Common use cases

  • Quick calculations and running totals
  • Tracking personal budget items
  • Small data tables without Excel
  • Summing expenses for a report
  • Simple pivot-like aggregations

How it compares

Compared to Google Sheets, Freesheets is lighter weight and doesn't require a Google account. It's not designed for heavy analytics or multi-user collaboration — for those, Sheets or Excel are better. For everyday "I need to add up some numbers" work, Freesheets is faster.

Privacy

Spreadsheet data stays in your browser. Export to CSV or XLSX to save externally.

Frequently asked questions

Is Freesheets free?

Yes. 100% free with no signup and no usage limits.

Which formulas are supported?

Common spreadsheet functions: SUM, AVG, COUNT, MIN, MAX, IF, VLOOKUP, CONCAT, LEN, ROUND, and many more.

Can I import an Excel file?

Yes. Open .xlsx files directly, or copy-paste a range from Excel or Google Sheets.

How large a sheet can Freesheets handle?

Up to a few thousand rows comfortably. For larger datasets, use Excel or Google Sheets.

Does it have charts?

Not yet. For charts, pair with Excel or export the CSV to a visualization tool.

Is there cross-device sync?

No. Export to CSV or XLSX and transfer manually for cross-device use.
