Frequently asked questions

Is Freetypingtest free?

Yes. Ad-free with no signup.

Which keyboard layouts are supported?

All layouts (QWERTY, Dvorak, Colemak, international). The test accepts any character you type correctly.

Is there a leaderboard?

Not currently. Personal best is saved locally.

Can I compare against global typing speeds?

Average typing speed is around 40 WPM. 60+ WPM is above average. Above 100 WPM is professional level.

Does it count punctuation?

Yes. Capitalization and punctuation are required to be typed correctly for accuracy tracking.

What passages does it use?

A mix of classic literature excerpts, common English phrases, and varied vocabulary for realistic practice.
